Why we’re running

This June, Kent Democrats have a rare choice on the ballot: a primary for our District Leaders on the Putnam County Democratic Committee. There are three seats and four candidates.

We are running together because the committee should reflect the values of the Democrats it represents. One of the four candidates, Louis Epstein, has been openly and repeatedly anti-LGBTQ+ — in letters to the editor and at county and town committee meetings. His presence has made it harder to recruit volunteers, earn the trust of local Democrats, and elect Democrats in Kent.

Everyone is entitled to their views. But leadership of the Democratic Party should not alienate the very neighbors we ask to join us. We believe Kent deserves a committee that welcomes everyone — and that’s the committee we intend to build.

We’re asking you to vote for the other three candidates, all current committee members in good standing who share our values as Democrats.

Questions & answers

When is the primary?

The Democratic Primary is Tuesday, June 23. Check your mail-in ballot or your local poll site for details. You can find early voting times and locations on the Kent Democrats voting page.

Who do I vote for?

The race for Member of County Committee asks you to vote for up to three candidates. We’re asking you to vote for Anna Walsh, Robin Fillhart, and Kathleen Valletta-McMorrow — everyone except Epstein.

What is the Putnam County Democratic Committee?

It’s the local body of District Leaders (also called Committee Members) who organize the Democratic Party at the town and county level — supporting candidates, petitioning, and connecting with voters. Learn more at Kent Democrats and Putnam Democrats.

Am I eligible to vote in this primary?

Registered Democrats in Kent Election District 01 can vote. ED1 covers the area roughly between East Boyd’s Nimham and Gipsy Trail Rd, up to Farmers Mills, and south to the end of Boyd’s reservoir. If you’re unsure, email us and we’ll help you check.

How can I help?

Tell friends and neighbors in ED1 about the primary, or email us — we’re a small, ragtag team and we welcome any help at all. We’ve reached our fundraising goal, but if you’d still like to chip in, you can do so through our ActBlue page.
